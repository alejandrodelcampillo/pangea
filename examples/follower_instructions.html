Oh no, you're in house full of dangerous traps! Fortunately, a friend has recorded spoken instructions to enable you to follow a safe path in the house. Please read ALL of the instructions before starting.

Playback

  • Note that after you press the begin button, your friend's spoken audio instructions will begin to play.
  • Listen to the instructions as you move, following the path as it is described.
  • Use the audio controls if needed, or press the ESC key to play and pause.
  • Follow the instructions as closely as possible. Do NOT explore the building unnecessarily and do NOT traverse the path multiple times unless you are lost.
  • You should NEVER be following your own instructions. Please tell somebody if this happens.

Movement

  • Look around the room by dragging the screen.
  • Move around the room by double-clicking. The YELLOW square indicates where you will move to next upon double-clicking.
  • When you believe you've reached the end of the path, press the submit button.
  • Both you and your friend will be evaluated by whether you were able to follow the correct path and reach the end without encountering any dangerous traps.
